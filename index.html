How to Play Word Master Game Online?

In this game, your objective is to guess the hidden word by typing letters or clicking the virtual keyboard. Based on your selected difficulty level, you will receive different numbers of hints and tries.

  • Easy:You get 8 tries and 2 hints.
  • Medium:You get 6 tries and 1 hint.
  • Hard:You get 6 tries and 1 hint.

To submit your guess, either type your word into the input field or click the letters on the virtual keyboard.

Difficulty Levels of Word Master Game

Choose the difficulty level before starting the game. The options are:

  • Easy:More tries and hints for beginners.
  • Medium:A balanced challenge with fewer tries and hints.
  • Hard:For experienced players who want a real challenge.

Hints of Word Master Wordle Game

Choose the difficulty level before starting the game. The options are:

  • Easy:You receive two hints during the game.
  • Medium:You receive one hint.
  • Hard:No hints are provided, making it more challenging.
Use of Virtual Keyboard

The virtual keyboard allows you to guess letters by clicking on them instead of typing. This feature can be especially helpful for younger players or those who prefer a more visual approach to guessing.

FAQs
1. How many words are there in the game?

There are 200+ words included, which consist of fruits, vegetables, and some general words, and each word is selected randomly for every game session.

2. What happens if I make an incorrect guess?

You will lose one try. If your tries reach zero, the game ends, and the correct word will be revealed.

3. Can I restart the game after I win or lose?

Yes, after the game ends, a restart button will appear, allowing you to play again.

4. How does the virtual keyboard work?

The virtual keyboard allows you to click letters instead of typing them. This can help you guess the word without needing to use the keyboard.

5. Is there a mobile version of the game?

Yes, the game is designed to be responsive and works well on mobile devices.

6. What if I need help with the game rules?

Refer to the "How to Play" section for detailed instructions. You can also check the FAQs for common questions.
